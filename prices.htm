Classes and private lessons are paid monthly and in advance. A typical 4 week month, attending one class per week or attending one private lesson per week would be R 260.00 and R 880.00 respectively.
Payment is gladly accepted in cash or by electronic banking.

 

Cancellation Policy

Cancelled classes are non refundable.

If 24 hours notice is given and if there is a space in another class you will be able to make up a missed class within that month.

Private lessons will be charged for unless 24 hours notice is given.

Should you be away or travelling for an extended period, full payment is required if you would like to retain your space in a class.

Should you wish to discontinue Pilates a 2 week notice period would be appreciated. This allows time for new clients to be prepared for class.

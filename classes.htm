Studio Etiquette

Pilates is a hands on method.

Comfortable, stretchy clothing is recommended. Shorts, leggings,
t-shirt and socks would all be suitable. During winter it is advisable to wear layers so that as the body warms up you can remain comfortable.

Mats are cleaned and packed away at the end of each session by participants to maintain hygiene.

Please ensure you arrive before the class is due to begin. Classes are planned with care to maximise the benefit to your body within the available time. Arriving late for class is unfair to other participants, the teacher and more importantly will compromise the benefit to your body.

Cell phones should be on silent at all times and if you need to take an emergency call, please take it outside of the studio.

There is safe parking within my property and I would encourage you to use it. If you choose to park on the road, please be considerate of the neighbours.

Justine at Just Pilates

Justine qualified in London as a Body Control Pilates instructor in 2005. She held matwork classes in Devon, England before moving back to South Africa in 2007.

 

Her home studio offers you matwork classes and private sessions incorporating the Reformer and other accessories.

In 1992 Justine completed a BCom at UCT and then went on to pursue a career in Fashion Retail. She worked as a merchandiser in the buying department of Truworths and then as a Supply Chain Manager for Laura Ashley’s Franchising Department in London. After the birth of her first son, corporate life lost its appeal and she began swimming teaching for all ages. At this point Pilates became part of her life and proved to be so beneficial to her own body, mind and spirit that she decided to persue Pilates teaching.

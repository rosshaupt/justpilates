Private Lessons

Private lessons are invaluable for all clients as
each session is designed to address your individual needs.
 

 

Specific problem-areas (the lower back, for example) can be targeted by an exercise, but always in relation to the rest of your body. Your body will be different to another and in an individual session your teacher will guide you on how best to gain control and ease of movement for yourself.

The Pilates Reformer as designed by Joseph Pilates is a fantastic piece of equipment which helps to target those difficult areas. Using tension springs and a movable carriage, your core muscles are challenged more extensively and difficult movements can be facilitated.

Private lessons last 1 hour and can be scheduled weekly, monthly or on an ad-hoc basis.
